Hi there!

My name is Haoyu (Richard) Zhuang, a second-year graduate student studying tropical weather at the University of Washington under the advisory of Professor Shuyi Chen.

I am broadly interested tropical weather and climate, weather-climate continuum, and machine learning.

I am currently working on the multiscale variability of the Atlantic ITCZ.
